Redesign the website for A&L Financial Solutions — a Hull-based financial advice firm
specialising in mortgages, life cover, and personal protection insurance. The adviser
is Anthony Thackeray. The firm is authorised and regulated by the FCA as an appointed
representative of PRIMIS Mortgage Network.

This is a full redesign, not a refresh. The current site uses a generic WordPress
theme with stock imagery and weak copy. The new site must look like it was built by
a specialist studio — clean, trustworthy, and premium without being cold.

SERVICE DETAILS TO USE:

LIFE COVER
- Pays a lump sum if you die during the policy term
- Can be used to pay off a mortgage, cover lost income, or support dependents
- Premiums depend on age, health, term length, and amount of cover
- A&L searches across a panel of insurers to find the right fit

CRITICAL ILLNESS
- Pays a tax-free lump sum on diagnosis of a specified serious illness
- Covers conditions like cancer, heart attack, and stroke (varies by provider)
- Can be used for treatment, adaptations to your home, or replacing lost income
- Separate from life cover — pays out while you're alive, not on death

INCOME PROTECTION
- Pays a regular income if you can't work due to illness or injury
- Covers a percentage of your pre-illness income until you return to work
- Deferred period and benefit period set when you take out the policy
- Often overlooked — statutory sick pay alone is rarely enough to live on

MORTGAGE BROKER
- Anthony searches the mortgage market on your behalf
- Access to a wide panel of lenders, including deals not available direct
- First-time buyers, home movers, and remortgages all covered
- Fee: between £457 and £997, agreed before any work begins, payable on completion

MORTGAGE PROTECTION
- Designed to pay off your outstanding mortgage balance if you die during the term
- Often set up as decreasing term insurance — cover reduces alongside the mortgage
- Can be combined with life cover or critical illness depending on your needs
- Ensures your family keeps the home if you're no longer there

HOME INSURANCE
- Covers buildings (the structure) and/or contents (your belongings)
- Buildings insurance: required by most mortgage lenders
- Contents insurance: covers theft, fire, flooding, and accidental damage
- A&L helps you find cover that matches what you actually own and need
